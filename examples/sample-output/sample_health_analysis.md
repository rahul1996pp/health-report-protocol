## 8. Location-aware food guidance (Anantapur / South-Indian) *(example)*
| Limit / smaller portions | Better local swap | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Large servings of **white rice** | **Millets** — ragi (finger), jowar, **korralu (foxtail millet)** | Slower sugar release; helps HbA1c & triglycerides |
| **Fried snacks** (mirchi bajji, punugulu, bonda) | Steamed **idli**, plain **dosa**, roasted peanuts | Less oil → lower triglycerides |
| **Sweets** (boorelu, sugary chai) | Fruit; unsweetened buttermilk (majjiga) | Less sugar → HbA1c, weight |
| Heavy coconut/oil curries | Dal, sambar, more vegetables/greens | Fibre + protein; lighter on lipids |

*Supportive, not "banned" — discuss with your doctor or a dietitian.*
